### ğŸ¯ How to learn them

The goal is to **memorize the sounds and strokes** of each character.

Don't worry if they seem difficult at first: with constant practice, you'll recognize them automatically.

Study tips:

- Listen to each syllable and repeat it out loud.
- Practice writing each character several times.
- Associate sounds with words you already know (like _sushi_ or _samurai_).
- Use flashcards or spaced repetition apps (like Anki or Quizlet).
